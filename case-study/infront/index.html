Infront

What rights maximization looks like across 10 sports properties

10
Sports properties
1000+
Events delivered annually
4
Languages in 2025

Infront manages some of the most complex rights portfolios in international sport. Across the 10 properties Infront works with Spalk on, the challenge is coordinating signal delivery, rights-compliant distribution and a growing multilingual output, all from a single operational hub.

Spalk integrates directly into Infront's Milan MCR, embedding into their existing master control infrastructure. That integration is what makes the workflow scalable with signals, coordination, and quality checks flow through a single point of contact, reducing the operational overhead of managing multiple properties simultaneously.

On game day, signals arrive via SRT and Spalk takes responsibility for distribution onward to TV networks, OTT platforms and social media streaming destinations. Each feed is routed according to the specific rights arrangement for that league. No two properties are identical, and Spalk’s system flexes to accommodate each one.

While the majority of feeds are English World Feeds, Infront is producing an increasing volume of multilingual commentary to unlock new markets and grow the underlying value of their rights. Spalk's Talent Marketplace provides the infrastructure to scale that output by identifying commentators by language, sport, and market familiarity, and managing contracting and payment in the same workflow.

For a rights management business, the commercial logic is straightforward: more languages mean more markets, and more markets mean more value. Spalk is how Infront is building that capability without building a new operation to run it.
