Talent Marketplace

Largest marketplace
of professional
commentary talent in the world

Spalk's Talent Marketplace gives rights holders direct access to the world's largest network of professional sports commentators. You can search by language, sport and experience, then audition, contract, and pay, all in one place.

Schedule a Demo

Talent on demand, quality guaranteed

Every commentator's environment and equipment is vetted for broadcast quality before they go live. Using the Marketplace, you can search by language, sport or experience level, audition candidates directly on the platform and have talent confirmed and ready well ahead of your event.

Feature

Structured access to global commentary talent

A vetted multilingual network with centralized booking, contracting, scheduling and payment management.

Audition before you commit

Listen to real commentary samples and review full experience profiles before booking, so you know exactly who you're putting on air.

Transparent global rates, one currency

See commentator rates across every market displayed in your preferred currency with no back-and-forth, no currency conversion and no surprises at invoice.

50+ languages, one search

Select by language, experience level, sport or availability and find broadcast-ready talent for any market your rights require.

Many voices, one invoice

Spalk manages the complexity of cross-border payments, freelancer details and schedule/invoice reconciliation. You broadcast in multiple languages around the globe pay a single, predictable  invoice.

benefits

Find the right voice for every audience

The right commentator makes every market feel local. Spalk gives you the network, the tools and the workflow to find them fast.

Authentic local voices

Deliver regionally relevant coverage with experienced local commentators who understand the language, culture and audience expectations in the market you serve.

Expand into new markets without new overhead

Adding a language used to mean finding an agency, negotiating a contract, and managing a new payment relationship. Spalk handles all of it so that reaching a new audience is a search and a click and not a procurement process.

Scale your commentary without scaling your team

Whether you're covering 5 games or 500, the workflow is the same. Spalk's Marketplace absorbs the operational complexity so your production team doesn't have to grow with your output.

Broadcast-ready tech standards

All commentators operate with tested equipment and stable connectivity, ensuring professional audio quality and uninterrupted delivery.
