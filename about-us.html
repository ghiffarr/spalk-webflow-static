about us

The platform behind the world's biggest sports broadcasts

Spalk is the cloud-based commentary platform trusted by the NFL, MLB, DAZN and the Bundesliga. We give broadcasters the tools to scale live commentary across any sport any language and any market - and we give commentators the platform to build a global career.

How Spalk came to life

We started because we saw a gap. Talented commentators existed everywhere, but broadcasters couldn't find them. The world's best voices stayed trapped behind language barriers and geography.
